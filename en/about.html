A message from our Representative Director

Back on a trip to China, I found myself in the sticks a whole hour's drive away from the nearest city. While I was there I found a full set of the manga 'Slam Dunk'. I often hear things like 'Japanese pop culture is popular all over the world', but this was an experience that made me feel just how true that phrase is.

Ever since I was a child, I've thought of anime, manga and games as being not just simple entertainment, but also as media that lets you learn new things, make new friends and develop as a person. (I recently even bought the whole set of Ultimate Muscle in the hopes it can teach my kids a thing or two).

Japanese pop culture is not just mere 'popular content', it really is magnificent culture.

There are surely people who feel this way not just in Japan, but all around the world. I want to help this magnificent culture reach more and more people, no matter their location.

This is an impossible task to do alone, but with your strength I want to build a service that will let this culture flourish in every corner of the world.

That is my mission.

Testuya Okamura, Representative Director of Otsukai, Inc
