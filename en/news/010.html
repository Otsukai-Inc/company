Otsukai joins Otakuthon 2019 as a sponsor

Otsukai participated in Otakuthon, the biggest anime and manga convention in Quebec. Otsukai donated 5 boxes of Japanese figures and Otsukai's signage could be found throughout the event, including a full-color full-page advertisement in the official guidebook handed out to the 25,000-plus attendees.
