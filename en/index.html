Vision / Missionビジョン / ミッション

Vision

For Otsukai to be the go-to for people across the globe to access items and information from Japan, and an easy way for real people in Japan to play their part in spreading Japanese culture.

Mission

Our love for Japanese pop culture drives us to spread its charms across the globe. We create a platform that lets us team up with others with the same goals, so that we can spread the joys of Japanese pop culture to places and people we wouldn't be able to on our own.
