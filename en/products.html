01

A cross-border marketplace specializing in Japanese pop culture

Visit Otsukai

From counterfeit goods to inflated prices, there are more than a few barriers to overcome when it comes to buying Japanese goods when you are overseas. The language barrier can also make it difficult to research what is out there. Otsukai is a service which brings overseas users on the hunt for genuine items together with Japanese users who can help them smash through those barriers. We hope that with Otsukai we can make it just as easy to get hold of Japanese merchandise outside Japan as it is within.
With users from more than 125 different countries, 80% of customers that make purchases come back for more.

Features
  • A marketplace centered on requests from individuals

    Otsukai is a marketplace where users outside of Japan create requests for the items they are looking for. Japanese users then search for those items and make offers to ship them directly to the buyers.

  • No need to worry about language

    Otsukai is developed and maintained by both Japanese and non-Japanese staff. It is designed in such a way that Japanese users need little English ability, and overseas users need no Japanese.

  • Safe transactions

    Otsukai ensures all transactions are conducted safely and securely for users on both sides by having the buyer pay in advance and keeping hold of the funds until the item has arrived.

  • Bilingual support

    In the unlikely event that trouble were to occur, we have Japanese and English-speaking staff ready to support users from either side.

02

An English-language source for anime news, reviews and interviews and more

Visit MANGA.TOKYO

MANGA.TOKYO is an English-language online news site which specializes in Japanese popular culture, with over 3 million views per month. News is just a small part of the site, with many interviews from legendary figures in the anime industry, event reports, anime reviews and other exclusive articles which can only be found on MANGA.TOKYO.

Features
  • Quality translation by lovers of Japanese pop culture

    MANGA.TOKYO both publishes articles originally written in English as well as articles translated from Japanese. Our in-house translators not only accurately translate Japanese articles into their native languages, but they are of course themselves lovers of Japanese culture.

  • Interviews with top anime directors, illustrators, voice actors, performers and more

    Not only does MANGA.TOKYO interview some of the top talent in the anime industry, we also collect questions from our readers via social media before the interviews take place, giving fans the chance to close the distance between them and their favourite creators.

  • Original projects such as merchandise and a light novel

    As well as collaborating with popular illustrators to create original merchandise sold at Comiket 91, MANGA.TOKYO has also collaborated with some great talent to produce its own original light novel, ‘Shikigami Girl’.

  • News articles translated directly from trusted Japanese sources

    As well as translating news sent directly from anime PR companies, MANGA.TOKYO also translates articles provided by trusted Japanese anime news sources such as AnimeAnime and Comic Natalie.
